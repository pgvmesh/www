<section>
  <h2 id="sites-wanted">Sites Wanted</h2>
  <p>If you have rooftop, tower, or hill-top access anywhere in Central North Carolina with a clear line-of-sight, reach out to <a href="mailto:info@pgvmesh.org">info@pgvmesh.org</a> or <a href="https://discord.gg/AUjz5RGGVu">join Discord</a>.</p>
  <ul>
    <li><b>All antenna & water towers:</b> these provide excellent elevation and coverage for the surrounding area.</li>
    <li><b>Any buildings or hilltops with 5+mi views:</b> while we want to grow the network wide, having in depth neighborhood coverage is critical especially during natural disasters.</li>
    <li><b>Tall buildings and office towers:</b> high-rise structures in urban centers are ideal for bridging different parts of the city.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
