    <h2 class="partner-heading" id="independent-details">The Independent Condos</h2>

    There are two nodes installed at The Independent, one on the northeast corner and one on the southwest. Because the building's exterior cage blocks RF signal and no roof or wall penetrations are allowed, custom mounting solutions with 3D-printed parts were designed to meet these constraints. Together, the nodes provide coverage across a massive portion of PGV, reaching as far as the surrounding communities and neighboring towns.
